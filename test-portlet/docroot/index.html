原材料采购价格总汇表 Raw Material Costs
常用钢材原材料采购价总表管理办法
1.由采购部汇总目前公司所有常用原材料资料并填写此表；
2.由采购部汇总所有研发/工程人员/各生产单位询价原材料相关资料并更新此表；
3.由采购部汇总所有原材料采购波动价格资料并更新此表；
4.由采购部汇总所有公司产品可能使用材料并补充此表；
5.由采购部按照以上四个方面填写此表，并定期维护，每2个月更新一次发公司业务部、核算部、研发部、总经办、董事长等；
6.原则上每种原材料须由采购部咨询后确定三家以上供应商，以备各单位选用时根据客户要求、图面要求、产品成本要求等进行筛选；
7.公司业务部、核算部、研发部、总经办、董事长等单位在产品估价、报价、原材料采购时均参照此表。当超出表中范围时须特别说明。
Frequently used steel raw material sheet for management purposes
1.Using data collected on commonly used raw material by materials purchasing department；
2.Using data collected through inquiries by all other production departments;
3.Using all data collected from procurement of raw materials, price fluctuations are updated；
4.Summary of all materials that may be used in company products are added to the table；
5.Purchasing will update this table once every two months in accordance with the above 4 points and will forward to company finance, sales, R&D, management, and top management;
6.For each material, procurement is consulted with at least 3 different suppliers，preparing the units in according customer specifications and product cost requirements；
7.All company departments will reference this table for estimation and quotation purposes。When a price is out of range,it must be investigate.
